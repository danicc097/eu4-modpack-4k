[u]Here you can find a list of all peace treaties, casus bellis, state edicts and diplomatic actions in the mod, with additional information.[/u]
The conditions for peace treaties, state edicts and diplomatic actions listed here are purely the ones needed for them to show up in the UI, whether there are additional requirements to select them will be shown in-game.

[h1][b][u]Peace Treaties[/u][/b][/h1]

[h1]Demilitarize Borders[/h1]

[b]Conditions for Visibility:[/b]
[list]
[*] any province of the loser has a fort and is in an area that borders a province of the winner
[/list]

[b]Effects:[/b]
[list]
[*] all provinces with a fort, which are owned by the loser and lie in an area that neighbors the winner's provinces, will lose the fort as well as 1 base manpower.
[*] the effect is executed 1 day after the peace deal is signed, to make sure it uses the new borders (make sure to take your province changes in the peace deal into account tho, so that the target after the deal is made still owns matching provinces)
[/list]

[h1]Demand Administrative/Diplomatic/Military Aides[/h1]

[b]Conditions for Visibility:[/b]
[list]
[*] loser's ruler has at least 3 ADM/DIP/MIL
[*] winner's ruler has less than 6 ADM/DIP/MIL
[*] winner's ruler has not yet received Administrative/Diplomatic/Military Aides (a ruler can get each once)
[/list]

[b]Effects:[/b]
[list]
[*] loser's ruler loses 1 ADM/DIP/MIL
[*] winner's ruler gains 1 ADM/DIP/MIL
[/list]
